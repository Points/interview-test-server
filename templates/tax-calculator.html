Tax Calculator Application

Assignment Description:

Points wants to build an income tax calculator to help show its users how marginal taxes work.
This calculator will take in a user's annual gross income and the year they are filing. After fetching the corresponding tax bracket through an API request, the calculator will output:

  1. The total amount they owe in federal income tax.
  2. The effective tax rate.
  3. A break down of tax rates and amounts for each applicable tax bracket,.
